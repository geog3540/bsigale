Brendan Sigale

I've always had a passion for problem solving. From troubleshooting my Grandma's dial-up modem to jailbreaking my first iPod touch at 10 years old, tinkering and using computers has always felt natural to me. Just as natural, then that I went on to study Business Analytics at the University of Iowa. I was drawn to the ability to use computers to interpret data and develop real solutions. Iowa's focus on practical industry experience served me so well that I went on to earn my Master of Science, Business Analytics through Iowa's Undergraduate to Graduate program.

I'm fluent in Python, SQL, and R and have experience in and exposure to virtually every side of an analytics stack. From database design and implementation to Deep Learning algorithms, I've experienced enough parts of analytics to add value in any situation.

My Skills

Database Design

I have over four years of experience designing and implementing databases. I've worked in tools such as Oracle APEX, PostgreSQL and PostGIS, and Big Data using Hive and Spark.

Identifying Opportunity

Every dataset has knowledge to offer. I'm experienced in analyzing datasets in Python, R, and Excel and creating metrics to identify insights leading to new opportunities.

Machine Learning

I've optimized and developed ML algorithms to solve problems using various tools, such as scikit-learn (both for quantitative and text data), Rattle, and Tensorflow.

Geospatial Analytics

Understanding spatial relationships can be the difference between success and failure. I've worked extensively in geospatial analytics using tools such as ArcPy, ArcGIS Pro, and Alteryx.
